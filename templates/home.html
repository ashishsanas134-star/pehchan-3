Pehchan

Your Unique Identity

Established in 2020, Pehchan-your unique identity is a non-profit organisation focused on the education of underprivileged children and helping the homeless.

OUR CAUSE

Celebrations

Throughout the year, we celebrate national and cultural festivals with underprivileged children and communities, spreading smiles and fostering togetherness.

Environment Protection

At Pehchan, we believe that taking care of the environment is essential to our mission of helping the underprivileged. We organize this by planting trees and cleaning up beaches, and by promoting environmentally friendly practices in our community.

Ration Distribution Drive

We distributed dry ration kits among the needy and destitute people in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai. This drive aimed to provide essential food items to those who require them the most.

Education for All

Spreading Awareness on Importance of Education

We believe that education is the key to unlocking a better future for the underprivileged. We conducted a campaign to spread awareness about the importance of education and motivated parents to send their children to school. We also spent quality time with the children, teaching them the basics and providing them with educational materials as per donations received. Our aim is to distribute basic educational items such as books, stationery, and uniforms to support the education of underprivileged children.
