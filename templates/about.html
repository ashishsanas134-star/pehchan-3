Pehchan

Your Unique Identity

We are a purpose-driven NGO committed to creating meaningful change at the grassroots level. Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities through access to education, empower women to lead independent and dignified lives, and provide essential relief to those in need. At the heart of our work lies a deep respect for nature — we actively engage in environmental preservation to ensure a sustainable future for generations to come. Through compassion, collaboration, and tireless dedication, we strive to build a world where equality, opportunity, and harmony with the Earth are not just ideals, but realities for all.

Who We Are

Our Vision

Here at Pehchan-Your Unique Identity, we know that sometimes all it takes to change the world is a little support. Since our start in 2020, we have been determined to make an impact. The core of our efforts is to bring our team's fresh ideas and passion to the range of activities we're involved in. PEHCHAN-YUI is working for social welfare as a team, which was formed by Aniksha Upadhyay and team in April 2020. It works on several causes Awareness, Children Education, Environment, Animals, helpless people and Community Welfare.

Our team is dedicated to service for poor public welfare and animal welfare.
